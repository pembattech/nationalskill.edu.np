ABOUT US

Welcome to National Skill Institute! We're in Bhaktapur, Suryabinayak, and we offer classes in Korean language and computer skills. Our goal is to help you become better at these things. In our Korean classes, you'll learn to speak, listen, read, and write. Whether you want to chat with Koreans or work in South Korea, our teachers will help you get there. We also have computer classes for all levels. From basic stuff to advanced coding, we'll teach you what you need to know to succeed in the tech world. Our teachers are great, and our school is modern and cool. Come learn with us and open up new opportunities for yourself!

Welcome to the National Skill Institute! Based in Bhaktapur, Suryabinayak, we specialize in Korean language and computer skills training. Our courses cover speaking, listening, reading, and writing Korean, along with computer topics from basic to advanced levels including graphic design and programming. Join us to boost your skills and explore new opportunities in language and technology.
